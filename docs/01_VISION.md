# Product Vision

## Mission
Help people discover clothing they actually want without scrolling through a static catalogue.

## Core promise
Open DRIPLY, swipe a few cards, and quickly find at least one relevant item from a real seller.

## Initial market
- Warsaw
- streetwear, sneakers, vintage and selected accessories
- independent resellers and small shops

## Positioning
DRIPLY is not a direct copy of Vinted. Vinted is search-first; DRIPLY is discovery-first.

## Product principles
1. Mobile first.
2. Visual before textual.
3. Fast path from discovery to seller conversation.
4. Recommendations must remain diverse.
5. Marketplace liquidity is more important than feature count.
6. Trust, moderation and clear item status are required from the start.
